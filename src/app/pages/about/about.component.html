About FIRST Robotics

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a global high school robotics program that combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. Teams of students build and program industrial-sized robots to compete in a themed challenge against other teams.

Founded by inventor Dean Kamen, FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) aims to inspire young people to become leaders in STEM fields through mentor-based programs that build engineering skills, teamwork, and innovation.
